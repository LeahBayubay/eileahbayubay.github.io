About Us
To ensure the credibility and expertise of our topic, our workshop will provide concrete, researched, fact-based information. In our workshop,
audiences will be strongly reassured that the data they have gained will
be used and implemented.
We, students, won’t be able to stop this pandemic in the blink of
our eyes, instead, we did think about how we spread awareness and
knowledge even if we can’t go outside. Because in our group,
we believe, if you are knowledgeable, risks are less.
